Lending Process

1

Click on Live Loans or Manual Lending from the Dashboard

Tap on the bottom navigation menu to go to Live Loans where you can see the list of all loans that are approved after thorough credit assessment.

Tip:

Live Loans shows you pre-approved loans ready for funding with complete borrower information.

2

Filter & Sort Borrowers

Customize your loan selection by filtering based on Tenure, Repayment Type, Risk Category, Income, and Loan Amount. Sort by LenDenClub Score, Interest Rate, or other criteria.

3

Select Loans to Fund

Select up to 150 loans at a time for optimal diversification and risk reduction. Use "Select All" for quick selection.

Tip:

Diversifying across multiple loans helps minimize risk and stabilize returns.

4

Select Amount to Fund Per Loan

Choose how much to lend in each loan, starting from ₹250. For 100 loans, fund a minimum of ₹25,000 for best diversification.

5

Make Payment

Complete payment using UPI, Net Banking, Debit Card, or bank transfer (IMPS/NEFT/RTGS) for larger amounts.

Note: Once payment is successful, funding will be complete in all selected loans.

Understanding Repayment Schedule

The timing of the first repayment depends on the chosen schedule and the loan's disbursal date.

1. Monthly (EMI) Repayment:

Disbursal Date First Repayment Starts Example
1st to 20th of the month The next month Loan disbursed on 2nd November → Repayments start 1st December
After the 20th of the month The month after next Loan disbursed on 21st November → Repayments start 1st January

2. Daily Repayment

For loans under the Daily Repayment plan, repayments typically begin within 7 days of loan disbursal.

Where Your Earnings Go

All repayments (Principal + Interest) are credited directly to your registered bank account.

RBI Regulation:

For your security and compliance, repayments cannot be credited to a wallet and must go directly to your bank account.
